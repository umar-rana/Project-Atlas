Pricing alternatives — memo

17 Sep 2025 · Sara R. · Q4 Launch

Three options for the Q4 launch pricing tier. The recommendation is Option B, with the grandfather clause for existing customers held for 6 months.

Context

The current Pro tier captures most of our paid usage but offers no headroom for the team-collaboration features @ Dana K. is shipping in Q4. Three approaches were evaluated against retention risk, perceived fairness, and revenue uplift.

ℹ
Decision needed by Friday. Marketing site copy is blocked on this; design has held a placeholder for two weeks.

Options

  1. Option A — Single tier increase. Raise Pro by 20%. Simplest; highest perceived-fairness risk for existing customers.
  2. Option B — Add Team tier. New $24/seat tier; Pro stays. Grandfather existing seats at current price for 6 months. Recommended.
  3. Option C — Usage-based. Per-task / per-event metering. High implementation cost; uncertain user reaction.

Implementation note

The pricing endpoint accepts a tier parameter and resolves grandfathered seats on the server. See:

POST /v2/billing/quote
{
  "workspace_id": "ws_3fA2",
  "tier": "team",
  "seats": 14,
  "grandfathered_until": "2026-04-17"
}
⚠
Open question: grandfather behavior when a workspace adds new seats during the 6-month window. @ Jamal M. to confirm with billing.
