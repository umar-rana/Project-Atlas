On the discipline of small surfaces

A productivity tool earns its place by removing friction, not by adding rooms. The interface should feel like a well-kept workshop — every tool within reach, nothing on the bench you didn't put there. Density is care.

When density slips, you start scrolling for things you knew the position of. Use ⌘K when the surface stops fitting the work.
